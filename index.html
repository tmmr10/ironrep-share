IronRep

Workout Tracker

A clean, focused workout tracker built for people who actually lift. Track every set, build your perfect training plan, and watch your strength grow over time — all stored locally on your device.

Features

⚖️

Track Every Set

Log weight, reps, and set type for every exercise. IronRep remembers your last performance so you always know what to beat.

📋

Custom Plans

Build training plans with 50+ exercises across every muscle group. Drag to reorder, set target sets, and start with one tap.

📈

Strength Charts

Watch your progress with clear charts for every exercise. Track personal records, volume, and workout streaks over time.

⏱

Guided Mode

Step through each exercise with the guided workout mode. Built-in rest timer keeps you on track between sets.

🔗

Share Plans

Share your training plans and workouts with friends via a simple link or file. They can import it with one tap.

🌐

Two Languages

Full support for German and English. Exercise names, UI, and all content — in your preferred language.

Why IronRep

Your Data Stays Yours

No account required. No cloud sync. No analytics. All your workouts, plans, and records are stored locally on your device. Uninstall the app, and everything is gone — because we never had it in the first place.

Built for Lifters

No social feeds. No AI coaches. No gamification. IronRep is a straightforward workout log for people who want to track their training and get stronger — nothing more, nothing less.

One-Time Purchase

IronRep is free with ads. Want them gone? A single in-app purchase removes ads forever. No subscriptions, no recurring fees. Pay once, train in peace.

Available Everywhere

IronRep runs on iPhone, iPad, and Android. Your workout history, personal records, and training plans — always in your pocket, ready when you are.

Privacy by Design

IronRep does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. No Firebase. No Mixpanel. No tracking of any kind. Your training data exists only on your device.
