IRONREP

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

IronRep is a workout tracking app developed by tmmr. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about data practices.

Data Collection

IronRep does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data is stored locally on your device only, including:

  • Your name (optional, for personalization only)
  • Training plans and exercises
  • Workout history including weights, reps, and sets
  • Settings (weight unit, theme, rest timer)
  • Custom exercise photos

Advertising

IronRep displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can disable personalized ads in your device settings (iOS: "Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising"; Android: "Google > Ads"). Ads can be permanently removed through an in-app purchase.

Plan Sharing

When you share a training plan, exercise data (names, sets) is encoded into a link. This link does not contain any personal information.

In-App Purchases

IronRep offers a one-time in-app purchase to remove ads. Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob - Advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple / Google - In-App Purchases

Analytics

IronRep does not use any analytics tools such as Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar. No usage data is collected.

Data Deletion

All data is stored exclusively on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data.

Children's Privacy

IronRep does not knowingly collect data from children. The ads shown are not personalized by default.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
